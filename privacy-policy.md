# Privacy Policy — Abu Star Diamond

Last Updated: May 2025

## Overview
Abu Star Diamond (com.goldsmith.billing) is a personal billing 
and inventory management app for internal use.

## Data Collection
- This app does NOT collect personal data from users.
- No data is shared with third parties.

## Google Drive Usage
- Google Drive is used only for backup and restore of app data.
- Only files created by this app are accessed.
- No Drive data is read, modified, or shared externally.

## Google Sign-In
- Used solely to authenticate the user for Drive backup access.
- No profile data is stored or transmitted.
